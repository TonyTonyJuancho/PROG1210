Customer Edit Customer Help:

1- In the Edit Page you can change any data from the Customer and Update it.


2- City and Province have their own dropdown list where you can pick the respective city or province.


3- Next to city and province dropdown list there is a "+" that will give you the option to add a city or province in case is not included in the dropdown


4- With the first table you can edit or add repair request to the customer.


5- With the second table you can edit or add every euipment the customer has brought to the store.
