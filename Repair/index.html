Repairs Note:

Ensure you have an approved repair request before creating repair data.


Repair Help:

1- In the Repair Page you can see the ID,Repair Notes, Warranty, Technician and Status.


2- You can click on any repair box and you will see all of their details, incluiding the repair request, and the parts that the technician is going to use.


3- With the search bar you can search a repair by the notes, sort them or group them.


4-If you want to add a new repair you can do it by clicing the "+Add new repair" button.
