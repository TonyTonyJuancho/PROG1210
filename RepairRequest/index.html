Repair Requests Customer Help:

1- In the Customer Page you can see the ID,Customer Full Name, email and phone number.


2- You can click on any customer box and you will see all of their details, incluiding previous repair request, equipments they have brought and add a new repair request.


3- By clicking on "+ Add new Customer" button you can add a new customer with all of their details.


4- With the search bar you can search a customer by their name, sort them or group them.
